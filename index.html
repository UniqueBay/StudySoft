Our Services

Private Lessons

We take an interdisciplinary approach to the teaching of Science subjects at the College and Advanced Level (UTME|JUPEB|IJMB). Such students can become masters of the respective Medical, Chemical or Engineering Fields that they later chose to major in.

Metacognition Training

We train Motivated Students in special methods and techniques that enable effortless Mastery of Academics. We teach them to take full advantage of the Current Technologies and recent findings in the Psychology of Learning. Such students go ahead to win interschool competitions and ace external examinations.

Online Classes

We have Tutors available for Online one-on-one Lectures with students who live in distant places. Such students can also benefit from the Expertise and Mentorship of our trained Tutors, no matter where they live.
